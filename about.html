Who are we?

The Dog Stop and Shop is a small e-commerce dropshipping business run by mother and daughter Dianne Cohen and Talia Grayson. Both are life long dog owners, who understand the need for top quality pup products. Through many different vendors, they have curated a shop full of modern contemporary items. Dianne and Talia want to emphasize through these products that your fur friend is not just a dog, they are a part of your family. They deserve the best of the best, and that reflects through their company.
